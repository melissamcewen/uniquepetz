I thought it might be fun to try to replicate IRL breeds with the original PF Magic breeds using selective breeding. This is what I came up for with "Italian Greyhound". Dachie head, bulldog ears, dali body/coat, great dane legs/feet/tail. They should breed true but watch out for random mutations that might cause bicolors to show up due to random mutations + daschie head. I used some of Vickie's original Italian Greyhound breed as a foundation. Vickie was a Petz hexer/selective breeder and her guides taught me so much back in the 00s.

Standard

  • Scales: 120
  • Eye color: Brown or black
  • Ears: Bulldog
  • Head: Dachshund
  • Body: Dalmatian
  • Legs: Great dane
  • Feet: Great Dane
  • Tail: Great Dane
  • Coat: Dalmatian or Dachshund
