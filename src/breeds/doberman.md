## Standard

- Scales: 157 (Great Dane)
- Eye color: Brown or black
- Ears: Great Dane (Cropped), Dalmatian (Uncropped)
- Head: Dachshund
- Body: Dalmatian
- Legs: Great dane
- Feet: Great Dane
- Tail: Poodle (Docked), Great Dane (Undocked)
- Coat: Any smooth coat
- Leg extension: 17 (Great Dane)
- Body extension: 22 (Labrador)
