## Standard

- Scales: 150
- Eye color: Dark
- Ears: Bulldog
- Head: Scottie
- Body: Dalmatian
- Legs: Great dane
- Feet: Chihuahua
- Tail: Sheepdog (long)
- Coat: Scottie
- Leg extension: maximum (100)
- Body extension: maximum (100)
