These can be bred selectively but all the dogz on this page have been [genexed](/genex)

## Standard

- Scales: 150
- Eye color: Brown or black
- Ears: Bulldog
- Head: Dachshund
- Body: Dalmatian
- Legs: Dalmatian
- Feet: Dalmatian
- Tail: Dalmatian
- Coat: Any smooth coated
